BASIC CRYPTOGRAPHY

Cryptography is a term used in computer science to describe secure information and communication techniques that use mathematical principles and a series of rule-based calculations known as algorithms to change communications in difficult-to-decipher ways. These deterministic algorithms are used for cryptographic key generation, digital signature, data privacy verification, online browsing, and private communications including credit card transactions and email. As information is so important, attackers are focusing their efforts on computer systems and open communication channels in order to steal sensitive data or disrupt essential information systems. As a consequence, cryptography is an essential element of today's world.
