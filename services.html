DOCUMENTATION

Cryptography is a term used in computer science to describe secure information and communication techniques that use mathematical principles and a series of rule-based calculations known as algorithms to change communications in difficult-to-decipher ways. These deterministic algorithms are used for cryptographic key generation, digital signature, data privacy verification, online browsing, and private communications including credit card transactions and email. Majorly there are three types of cryptography and are Symmetric Key Cryptography (Private/Secret Key Cryptography), Asymmetric Key Cryptography (Public Key Cryptography) and Hash Function.
